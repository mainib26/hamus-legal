Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Hamus ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Hamus mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. You may optionally provide a phone number, profile photo, bio, location, and social media links.

Listing Content: When you create marketplace listings, we collect the photos, descriptions, prices, and location information you provide.

Messages: We store messages you send to other users through the App's messaging feature to deliver them to the intended recipients.

Device Information: We may collect push notification tokens, device type, and operating system version to deliver notifications and improve the App.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your approximate location to show nearby listings. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your marketplace listings to other users
  • To facilitate messaging between buyers and sellers
  • To process payments between users via Stripe
  • To send push notifications about messages, likes, and activity
  • To show relevant, nearby listings based on your location
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase: Database hosting, authentication, and file storage (hosted in the United States)
  • Stripe: Payment processing for marketplace transactions. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy
  • Expo: App delivery and over-the-air updates

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we anonymize your profile data and remove your listings. Some data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the App's profile settings
  • Update your information at any time in your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data through the App's settings
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Revoke location permissions through your device settings

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), secure authentication, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:

Email: support@hamusmarket.com

የአካባቢ ውሂብ: በፈቃድዎ ቅርብ ልጥፎችን ለማሳየት ግምታዊ አካባቢዎን እንሰበስባለን። በማንኛውም ጊዜ በመሣሪያዎ ቅንብሮች ውስጥ የአካባቢ ፈቃድ ማጥፋት ይችላሉ።

ሓሙስ ("ንሕና" ወይ "ናትና") ናይ ሓሙስ ሞባይል መተግበሪ ("መተግበሪ") ይመሓድር። እዚ ፖሊሲ ምስጢራውነት ነቲ መተግበሪ ክትጥቀሙ ከለኹም ሓበሬታኹም ብኸመይ ከም ንእክብ፣ ንጥቀም፣ ነፍልጥን ንሕሉን የብርህ።

1. ዝንእክቦ ሓበሬታ

ናይ መለያ ሓበሬታ: መለያ ክትፈጥሩ ከለኹም ስምኩም፣ ኢመይል ኣድራሻኹምን መሕለፊ ቃልኩምን ንእክብ። ብተወሳኺ ቁጽሪ ተሌፎን፣ ናይ መግለጺ ስእሊ፣ ብዛዕባኹም፣ ኣከባቢን ናይ ማሕበራዊ ሚድያ ሊንክታትን ክትህቡ ትኽእሉ።

ናይ ልጥፍ ትሕዝቶ: ናይ ገበያ ልጥፋት ክትፈጥሩ ከለኹም ዝሃብኩሞ ስእልታት፣ መግለጺታት፣ ዋጋታትን ናይ ኣከባቢ ሓበሬታን ንእክብ።

መልእኽትታት: ብናይ መተግበሪ መልእኽቲ ባህሪ ንኻልኦት ተጠቀምቲ ዝልእኩሞ መልእኽትታት ንታለሙዎም ተቐበልቲ ንምብጻሕ ንዕቅብ።

ናይ መሳርሒ ሓበሬታ: ማሳወቕታታት ንምብጻሕን መተግበሪ ንምምሕያሽን ናይ ፑሽ ማሳወቕታ ቶከናት፣ ዓይነት መሳርሒን ስሪት ኦፐሬቲንግ ሲስተምን ክንእክብ ንኽእል።

ናይ ኣከባቢ ዳታ: ብፍቓድኩም ቀረባ ልጥፋት ንምርኣይ ግምታዊ ኣከባቢኹም ንእክብ። ብዝኾነ ግዜ ኣብ ናይ መሳርሒኹም ቅንብራት ናይ ኣከባቢ ፍቓድ ከተጥፍኡ ትኽእሉ።

4. ሓበሬታ ምዕቃብ

5. መሰላትኩም

ነዞም ዝስዕቡ መሰላት ኣለኩም:

  • ምርካብ: ብናይ መተግበሪ መግለጺ ቅንብራት ናይ ውልቂ ዳታኹም ምርካብ
  • ምሕዳስ: ብዝኾነ ግዜ ኣብ መግለጺኹም ሓበሬታኹም ምሕዳስ
  • ምድምሳስ: ኣብ ናይ መተግበሪ ቅንብራት መለያኹምን ዝተተሓሓዘ ዳታን ምድምሳስ
  • ምቁራጽ: ኣብ ናይ መሳርሒኹም ቅንብራት ናይ ፑሽ ማሳወቕታታት ምቁራጽ
  • ምስራዝ: ኣብ ናይ መሳርሒኹም ቅንብራት ናይ ኣከባቢ ፍቓዳት ምስራዝ

6. ድሕንነት ዳታ
